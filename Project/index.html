About IIITA

Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad, is a premier institute fostering innovation in information technology and engineering education. Established in 1999, it continues to produce top-tier professionals and cutting-edge research.

Learn More

Programs Offered

IIITA offers a diverse range of programs designed to equip students with technical expertise, research skills, and industry-ready knowledge. Explore the details below:

  • B.Tech in Information Technology

    Key Highlights: Core courses in Data Science, AI, and Cloud Computing, with hands-on training through labs and internships.

  • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering

    Key Highlights: Focus on embedded systems, circuit design, and advanced labs for signal processing.

  • M.Tech in IT and ECE

    Key Highlights: Elective options, research-driven curriculum, and industry collaboration opportunities.

  • PhD Programs

    Key Highlights: Access to research facilities, global collaborations, and mentorship from experienced faculty.

IIITA’s programs are designed to bridge the gap between theoretical learning and practical applications, ensuring students are ready to excel in the fast-paced tech-driven world.

Placements

Placements at the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad (IIITA) are a hallmark of its academic and industry excellence. The institute has consistently maintained an outstanding placement record, attracting top recruiters from across the globe.

Placement Highlights

100% Placement Assistance: IIITA is committed to ensuring every eligible student secures a job opportunity in reputed organizations.

Highest Packages: The institute witnesses competitive salaries every year, with the highest package often crossing ₹1 crore for international placements.

Average Package: The average annual CTC offered to students consistently reflects the quality of education and industry relevance of the programs.

Top Recruiters: IIITA hosts global giants like:

  • Tech Companies: Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Adobe, Meta (Facebook), Nvidia, and Uber.
  • Consulting Firms: McKinsey & Company, Deloitte, and KPMG.
  • FinTech Firms: Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan Chase, and Morgan Stanley.
